The Schedule

Six weeks of football from the opening whistle in Mexico City to the final at MetLife Stadium.

Group Stage
June 11 – July 2, 2026
12 groups of 4 teams play a round-robin. Top two from each group advance. 72 matches across all 16 host cities. Every qualified nation plays at least 3 games.
Knockout Rounds
July 4 – July 14, 2026
Round of 32 → Round of 16 → Quarter-finals → Semi-finals. The new expanded format means more upsets, more drama, and more nations than ever getting a taste of knockout football.
The Final
July 19, 2026
MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford, New Jersey. Capacity 82,500. The biggest stage in football. Kickoff TBD — expect a Sunday afternoon slot for maximum global audience.
